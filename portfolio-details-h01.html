Orchid Plantation Maintaining System

This project was developed to maintain watering and fertilizing of an orchid plantation automatically in order to have an healthy growth of the plants. My role here was to design and develop motor mechanism and structure in this project. This projected was developed using C, Atmega32, and Atmel Studio. It was developed as an first year hardware project at the University.
